About Me

I’m an IT undergrad (Class of 2025) passionate about problem-solving, software development, and AI. My interests include biomedical image analysis, object recognition in digital images, and ensuring fairness in AI systems. Beyond my technical pursuits, I’m deeply committed to fostering community growth and knowledge-sharing.

I founded GraceKonnect, a community dedicated to connecting students through technical sessions, inspiring talks, and collaborative learning. My mission is to create a supportive space where students can learn, grow, and build innovative projects together.
